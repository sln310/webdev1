• Don't expect stability
  • Change is evident in life. Research shows that people who embrace change tend to adapt better in all circumstances. And they also see volatility as an opportunity rather than a crash.
  • Become stronger
  • Strong people recover faster from upheaval. You will be stronger when you set your goals in life. Or you can ask for the support of friends, family, and loved ones. Picking up a hobby or learning a new skill also helps a lot.
  • Be optimistic
  • This doesn't mean you have to always think well of the bad things that happen in your life. But you need to learn lessons from difficulties and stumbles from which to draw lessons for the next time.
